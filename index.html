About TRIP NFT

TRIP NFT was born out of a desire to think of a world without boundaries. Thanks to cryptocurrency, the traditional world as we know it is long gone. TRIP NFT is focused on freedom. Humans have always been attracted to the irresistible principle of freedom, but technology is finally allowing us to inch closer to a much freer, and more fluid, world. We fully believe that TRIP NFT can fulfill this desire, and genuinely believe that NFTs provide a sense of shared ownership and community that didn’t previously exist. Whether you’re new to crypto or a seasoned NFT investor: TRIP allows you to be a part of a globetrotting community with shared values.
